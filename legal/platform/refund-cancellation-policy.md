## 1. Scope

This policy applies to **subscriptions you pay to Peymiz** for access to our B2B SaaS platform.

It does **not** govern refunds between your business and **your customers**. Peymiz does not process payments on behalf of your customers and is not responsible for product refunds, order cancellations, or appointment disputes with your end customers. Those matters are solely between you and your customers.

## 2. Monthly Subscriptions

Peymiz subscriptions are billed on a **recurring monthly basis** (or as otherwise stated at checkout) until you cancel.

## 3. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by emailing **shilohdhd1@gmail.com**.

When you cancel:

- **Future renewals stop** — you will not be charged for subsequent billing periods
- Access typically continues until the **end of your current paid billing period**, unless your account is terminated earlier for breach of our Terms
- Cancellation does not delete your account automatically; you may request account deletion separately

## 4. Refunds

**Payments already made are generally non-refundable**, including partial months, unless:

- Required by applicable law in your jurisdiction, or
- Approved by Peymiz on a **case-by-case basis** (e.g., duplicate charge or verified billing error)

If you believe you were charged in error, contact **shilohdhd1@gmail.com** within **14 days** of the charge with your account email and transaction details.

We do not provide refunds for:

- Change of mind after a billing period has started
- Unused time during an active billing period
- Account suspension or termination due to violation of our Terms of Service

## 5. Free Trial

If you start a **free trial**:

- You will not be charged during the trial period described at signup
- You may cancel **before the trial ends** to avoid conversion to a paid subscription
- If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your selected plan may begin billing automatically according to the terms shown at signup

## 6. Price Changes

If subscription prices change, we will provide notice as described in our Terms of Service. Price changes apply to future billing periods after notice.

## 7. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback without contacting us first, we may suspend your account while the dispute is investigated.
